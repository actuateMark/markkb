- **Quartz / 11ty / Caddy quick-ref** (now in the plan note): Quartz `enableSPA: false` is required for subpath deploy; `EleventyHtmlBasePlugin` double-prefixes — don't load it with 11ty's `pathPrefix`; Caddy `handle_path` strips prefix, `handle` does not — pick by whether the proxy target already knows the subpath; more-specific handles MUST come first in the file.
