## Notes / Learnings

### Verifier / video-clip "no alerts found" (Vini investigation)

- **Mechanism (evidence-backed via NR + code trace):** admin-view alerts = `WindowIdsV2` DDB rows, written by `MultiAlertSender.trigger_alert → put_detection_window` **before** the dispatch gate (so "admin-only" persistence is the design — recipients not required). The row requires the sliding window to reach its **alert threshold** (`thresh=2` confirmed frames), not just to open. `create-detection-window` (k8s) logs "no alerts found" when no row exists.
- **Why no alert:** on connector-34952 (default `VideoCameraStream`/`VerifierCamera` path, slicing engaged at 2.8 fps), the pistol was detected on ~10 frames (conf 13–84) but the site's gate is **`pistol>80`/`gun>80`** — only the @84 frame cleared it → "confirmed frame count: 1 of 2" → window closed at clip EOF without firing.
- **Root cause = stale threshold:** settings.json shows `metrics.pistol.sensitivity=Medium` but `raw_metrics.pistol.minimum_confidence=80`. Neither documented Medium maps to 80 (weapon-v8 calibration: Medium=0.60; inference-API `thresholds.py`: Medium pistol=0.75) — 80 is the legacy v5 flat value. At a correct gate (≤66) the clip fires 3–4 confirmed frames.
- **Ruled out:** robo/gauntlet path (it ran default mode); missing slicing (slicing confirmed engaged); no-recipient suppression (persist precedes dispatch gate); video-provisioning gap (shares RTSP plumbing).
- 🚩 **Fleet escalation (possibly safety-relevant):** NR shows ~half the production weapon fleet still on the `>80` gate (other half on `>60`) → real weapons detected at 60–80% confidence may be **silently not alerting** fleet-wide. Likely an incomplete weapon-v8 threshold/sensitivity-mapping migration. Tracked in mark-todos "Tracked as relevant"; needs admin-DB/provisioning audit + DS sign-off on the intended v8 gate.
