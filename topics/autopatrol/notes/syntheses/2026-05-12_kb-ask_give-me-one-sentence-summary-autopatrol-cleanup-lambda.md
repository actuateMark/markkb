# give me a one-sentence summary of how the autopatrol cleanup lambda decides to d

## Question

give me a one-sentence summary of how the [[autopatrol-cleanup-lambda|autopatrol cleanup lambda]] decides to disable a site

## Answer

The cleanup lambda disables a site when its DDB counter — incremented every time the connector emits a "no patrols to run" SQS signal for that schedule — reaches the threshold `N = max(3, CLEANUP_TARGET_HOURS / cadence_hours)` AND a confirmation call to `AutoPatrolAPI.get_schedule` returns 404 or a non-active status (Suspended / Paused / Removed / Deleted); if Immix still reports Active/Awaiting, the counter is anomaly-reset instead.
