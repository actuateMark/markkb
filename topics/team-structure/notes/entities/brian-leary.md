# Brian Leary

Brian Leary is the **Product Lead** at Actuate, responsible for product-level decisions, strategic direction, and PRD authorship. He is the primary author of the [[watchman/_summary|Actuate Watchman]] PRD and drives pricing and packaging strategy across the platform.

## Product Leadership

Brian owns the product vision and prioritization framework that shapes engineering work across all initiatives. His decisions determine which features move from concept to roadmap, how initiatives are sequenced, and what tradeoffs are acceptable. He works closely with [[laura-reno]] (PM lead) on requirements and specifications, but retains final authority on product-level direction.

## Watchman PRD

Brian authored the Product Requirements Document for [[watchman/_summary|Actuate Watchman]], Actuate's next-generation operator dashboard. The [[watchman-repo|Watchman]] PRD defines the core product thesis: that monitoring center operators need a unified interface combining alert triage, camera health, patrol status, and dispatch workflows. The PRD establishes the [[multi-agent-architecture]] as the technical foundation and defines the [[triage-gamification]] and [[onboarding-wizard]] features as key differentiators. The PRD is the upstream document that drives the MVP requirements and agent specs produced by [[laura-reno]].

## Pricing and Strategy

Beyond feature definition, Brian drives Actuate's pricing and packaging strategy. This includes decisions around per-camera pricing tiers, add-on feature packaging (e.g., AutoPatrol as a premium tier, VLM capabilities as an upsell), and competitive positioning against other AI-powered video analytics platforms. Pricing decisions directly influence which features are prioritized on the [[product-roadmap/_summary|Product Roadmap & Initiatives]] -- features that drive revenue expansion receive higher priority. The [[revenue-drivers]] concept captures the metrics Brian tracks.
